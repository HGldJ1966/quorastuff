I once had a beautiful cursive handwriting, evidence - Multiple awards for best hand writing or whatsoever in primary school. Then, slowly I fell in the trap of academics and to increase my speed, I gave up on cursive slowly.

Now my handwriting is perfectly legible and artistically okay-ish, bingo- not the cursive style.

However, to regain the lost love, this is what I would do in future.

  • Buy those practice books again.
  • Start practicing on white sheets, on a regular basis
  • Take calligraphy classes (This is on the extreme edge, but satiates me)
  • Work out on the Thumb-Index finger positions. That matters a lot when it comes to a fast writing !
  • Most importantly, pick up the right Gel pen, which is often the savior for the cursive style. Or the bold ball pens, which have a friction less smooth rolling tip.
